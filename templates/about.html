Connecting the IBDP community

The Kizuna Initiative

The Kizuna Initiative is a proposed self-governing, student-led committee designed to unite and empower the IBDP student community through structured collaboration, academic enrichment, and meaningful service.

What We Aim to Achieve:

  • Coordinate CAS Experiences
  • Launch competition-oriented Clubs
  • Organize Community Events

We bring together people who want to build things, share ideas, and turn the IB experience into something more than just exams and deadlines.

Kizuna (絆)

Kizuna (絆) is a Japanese word meaning 'bonds between people'. Our initiative is about creating bonds across the IBDP community. Connecting students working on similar CAS projects, linking passionate learners through subject clubs, and building lasting relationships through shared events.
