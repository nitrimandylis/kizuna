About Kizuna

Connecting the IBDP community

What is Kizuna?

Kizuna (絆) means "bonds between people" in Japanese. It represents the connections that tie us together—friendships, shared experiences, and collaborative achievements.

Our Mission

We're building a platform that makes it easier for IBDP students to coordinate CAS experiences, discover and join clubs, and organize community events. No more scattered spreadsheets or missed opportunities.

Why It Matters

The IBDP is challenging. Having a community that supports you—through shared CAS projects, study groups, and meaningful events—makes all the difference. Kizuna helps you find your people.
